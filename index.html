As you would expect our electrical test, installation and maintenance work is conducted in accordance with IEE and British Standards, BS7671 Wiring Regulations.

Laser Electrical Services is also an NICEIC registered Domestic Installer, which means we are certified compliant against latest building regulations (Part P). In short, this means we are capable of 'self certification' against design, test and installation within domestic electrical installations. It also provides our customers added confidence, as all of our work is certificated and documented through the NICEIC.
